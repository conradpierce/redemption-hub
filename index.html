Execution Workflow
Step 01
Market Context Bias
Open A-Trader's Market Scanner Pro. Filter to your 4 pairs. The scanner flags trends, reversals, breakouts, and high-conviction setups. If nothing is flagged — no trade today.
Step 02
Key Level Identification
Pull up the flagged pair. Look left — where did price reverse before? Draw one horizontal line. That's your support or resistance.
Step 03
Risk-Off Execution
Use the RR tool: entry at the level, SL on the other side, TP at 2× the stop distance (1:2 RR). Confirm risk ≤ $1,000 (1%). Full screen. Execute. Walk away.

QBanks' Three Principles
01
Detach From Outcome. The trade is a statistical event. Your ego has no place in the probability curve. "Never make giving up an option."
02
Respect The Stop. Capital preservation is the only way to remain in the game. Money is a tool, not an emotion.
03
Zero Bias State. React to what the market is doing, not what you want it to do. Trade the chart, not the money.
🪞 Pre-Trade Gut Check
Am I chasing a move or following a programmed setup?
If the answer isn't "setup," don't trade.
Am I showing up regardless of yesterday's result?
If no — sit out. Resilience means showing up clean.
Am I trading the chart, not the money?
If you're thinking about P&L, reset. The money follows the discipline.
💰 The Withdrawal Moment
